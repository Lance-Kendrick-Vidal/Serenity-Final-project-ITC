About Me

Hi, I'm Lance Vidal, a caffeine-fueled travel enthusiast with an insatiable passion for uncovering the Philippines best known secrets and most beloved cafe destinations. I was Born in Mandaluyong but raised in Cavite, I've developed a deep affection for the province's warm hospitality, rich flavors and vibrant culture, which sparked my quest to explore the local cafe scene.

Growing up, I was captivated by the aromatic scents wafting from traditional coffee shops and the cozy ambiance of family-owned cafes. This innate curiosity led me to venture beyond Cavite's borders, discovering hidden gems in Tagaytay, Amadeo, Dasmariñas and surrounding towns.

My Story

Brews and Views Philippines started as a personal blog, where I shared my experiences and reviews of cafes in Tagaytay, Amadeo, and Dasmariñas. Today, it has grown into a comprehensive guide, featuring:

  • Honest cafe reviews
  • Vibrant photography
  • Exclusive interviews with cafe owners
